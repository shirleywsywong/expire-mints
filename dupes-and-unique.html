-------Find repeats-------

1. Write a function that takes two arrays and returns their intersection as an array.

intersection([1, 2 ,3], [2, 3, 4]) -> [2, 3]

intersection([a, 1 , 'foo'], ['foo', 17, a]) -> [a, 'foo']

2. Then write a function that takes two arrays and returns their non-intersection as an array.

unique([1, 2 ,3], [2, 3, 4]) -> [1, 4]

unique([a, 1 , 'foo'], ['foo', 17, a]) -> [1, 17]
